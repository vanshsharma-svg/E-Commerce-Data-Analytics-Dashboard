## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow by collecting e-commerce data from the **DummyJSON API**, cleaning and transforming it using **Python (Pandas)**, merging multiple datasets into a unified analytical dataset, and building an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** for business insights.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- 🚗 Vehicle category generated the highest revenue (**$1.88M**).
- 🛒 Cat Food was the highest-selling product by quantity.
- 🍎 Apple had the largest product catalog.
- 💰 Addison Wright was the highest-spending customer.
- 👥 Customer purchases were almost equally distributed between males and females.
- 🎯 Customers aged **27–35** were the most active buyers.
- ⭐ Most product ratings ranged between **3.5–4.8**.
- 💎 Premium categories contributed the highest revenue.
- 📦 High-demand products require better inventory planning.
- 📢 Loyalty programs can improve customer retention.
